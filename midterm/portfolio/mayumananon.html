To bring to the community creativity and happiness by the Mayumana's Language Through rhythm, movement, singing, acting and, humor, the organization connects with children and youth at risk, the emotional and/or physically disabled and the socially disadvantaged. In addition, it serves the community through multi-cultural activities and projects designed to improve the self-esteem of people of all ages. Projects using the language of Mayumana as a therapeutic tool give supportive encouragement and promote rehabilitation throughout the community.
