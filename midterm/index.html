About me

I'm an independent creative artist based in New York. With skills ranging from sound design, video editing to producing and composing music, you will not have to look for a product team as I can be your one-man creative studio that is sure to save your time and money but still delivers quality service. ​ As a sound designer, I draw creativity and precision from years of multi-disciplined roles within the music industry. Within the last decade, I have worked with Israeli and international clients as a freelance composer and video editor; composing on commercials, music videos, dance performances and other media.
